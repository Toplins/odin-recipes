Fat Cookies

Yummy And Gooey Fat Chocolate Chip Cookies

These are my favorite cookies to eat. They are huge and really hit the spot. I usually eat tons of cookies right away when I make them, but I can truly only eat one of these before I have had enough. They're oh so satisfying!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter cubed
  • ¾ cup light brown sugar
  • ¼ cup turbinado raw sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups flour white
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chunks or semisweet
  • ½ cup walnuts optional

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or non-stick spray.
  2. Cream together the butter and both sugars.
  3. Next stir in egg, egg yolk and vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder and salt.
  5. Start to fold the flour mixture into the sugar mixture until thoroughly combined.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips and walnuts.
  7. Scoop into even sized balls and place on baking sheet.
  8. Refrigerate dough for 2 hours or freeze for 90 minutes
  9. Bake for 9-12 minutes for medium sized cookies or 11-13 minutes for larger cookies.
